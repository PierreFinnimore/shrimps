      FAQ

      1. Do I have to be a student to join? No! Anyone is welcome!
      2. Do I have to pay money for the workshops? No! Anyone is welcome!
      3. Do I have to be funny? No! Anyone is welcome! We're all learning how to be funnier!
      4. Do I have to perform at the workshops? No! Participation is voluntary; you can watch and have a laugh!
